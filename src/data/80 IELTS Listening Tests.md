## Introduction
“80 IELTS Listening Tests” has been prepared by a team of qualified teachers at Proper
English School to help students overcome their difficulties with the IELTS LISTENING
## Test.
There are 80 Tests, containing 10 questions, which are designed to equip future IELTS
candidates with the necessary skills useful for this section of the test. The first 20 Tests
are for Section 1, the next 20 Tests are for Section 2, the third 20 Tests are for Section
3, and the last 20 Tests are for Section 4.
The book also contains a scoreboard where you can track your progress day by day,
and compare today’s result to the previous ones.
For each test, you will find the answer keys at the end of the book.
To Download the Audios, you can scan the QR code at the back page of the book, or
search on telegram by “80 Listening Tests”
